— ABOUT

I think best on grey-sky days, the kind that hold their breath until eight, then split open into gold, like the day had been saving something to say. My real thinking happens speeding through mountains in the rain.

Sometimes on a sixty-degree afternoon — sun threading through leaves, smoke in my lungs, the ground holding my weight — the universe feels like a single continuous thing. Birth and exhale and shadow, all running on the same rule. I try to build software with that feeling somewhere in the room. Tyagaraja's work moves me, so does jamaica pond, beantown.

I take things apart to understand them - pure distrust (jk), but because every explanation is smaller than the thing it points to. First principles, every time. What rule does this actually obey, and what happens if I sit with it long enough to find out?

I'm self-taught by way of reverse engineering — there is a pattern to entropy, everything is a math function in this (damn) universe..

Data scientist is the label that fits in the box. In practice I work in whatever the problem needs — all the unglamorous middle of the stack that decides whether something ships or is just a failed business decision. AI is the new steam engine (howdy howdy, more datacenters). Not efficient 🤑. Definitely not magic 🧛🏻‍♂️. If it costs too much or runs too slow, it isn't done.

If you're building infrastructure for AI systems that need to work in production, and our values align — talk to me.

Welcome to the new industrial revolution.

Reach me at avarutla@gmail.com, or find me on GitHub.
